SOL Grid Bot Strategy
The Solana day trading strategy using the Grid bot is ideal for sideways markets. It automatically sets and maintains a grid of buy and sell orders within a specified range. When a buy order is executed, the bot places a corresponding sell order above. Likewise, when a sell order is executed, a buy order is placed below. The Grid bot runs continuously until manually stopped or reaches the predefined profit limit. Choose from Long, Short, or Neutral Grid bot modes for any market situation. Long mode will initially place buy orders and Short mode will start with placing sell orders. In Neutral mode, bot will initially place sell orders above market price and buy orders below it.
